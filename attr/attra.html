Plaza Magalang

The Magalang Town Plaza, a symbol of community spirit and endurance, features a grand gazebo for cultural performances and music concerts, blending the past with the present, preserving memories for future generations.

14th Station

Banal na Bunduk, a popular tourist attraction in Orchard Village, Magalang, Pampanga, features Fourteen Stations of the Cross, completed in 2015 by Soroptimist International. The site offers religious and cultural significance, peaceful surroundings, and spiritual practices.

Batyawan

Batyawan Farm in Magalang, Pampanga offers a serene escape from urban chaos, featuring four green fields, curved hills, and diverse plants and animals. It promotes sustainable agriculture, environmental protection, and rural tourism in the Philippines, offering fresh organic food and meditation techniques.
